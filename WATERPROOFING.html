Shield Your Home With Expert Waterproofing Services

Damp walls, hairline cracks and leakages are villains to innocent homeowners like you and your cozy abode. In times like these, Nerolac Perma comes to your rescue. Nerolac Perma is a strong and reliable waterproof coating and an answer to all your leakage problems, cracks and water seepage issues. Such problems even arise in areas like bedrooms, bathrooms and kitchens due to weather conditions or humidity. Nerolac Perma is a holistic waterproofing solution designed with the latest technology and long-lasting results.

How to Waterproof Your Walls?

Rains can wreak havoc on your interior and exterior walls. Wall seepage, water stains, hairline cracks and dampness can be problems that lead to overall damage to your home’s structural integrity. Our expert painters and expert waterproofing solutions are designed to waterproof your walls with additional waterproofing services and a hassle-free experience. Following are the basic steps followed to waterproof your home and our experts can carry it out for you without a worry.

Prepare the Surface

Our expert NXTGen painters will visit the site and evaluate the area. After that, our experts will cleanse the area, bridge cracks and prepare the substrate for a fresh coat of Perma No Damp waterproofing paint.

Prime and Putty

Using our expert range of Nerolac Perma’s Waterproofing Putty and primers, the professionals will prime and ready the area, be it interior or exterior for paint. Nerolac Perma Magic Boost can be mixed in with the putty or primer to enhance the performance.

Application

Based on the expanse of your wall, our experts will use a brush or a roller for the waterproofing paint. Nerolac Perma No Damp and No Damp+ are ideal waterproof coatings for surfaces and Nerolac Perma Damp Protect Exterior is ideal for external horizontal surfaces.
